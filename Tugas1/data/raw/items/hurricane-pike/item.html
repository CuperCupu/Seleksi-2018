Hurricane Pike
4,615
+ 13 Intelligence
+ 2.0 HP Regeneration
+ 20 Agility
+ 15 Strength
Active: Hurricane Thrust
Pushes you and target enemy 450 units away from each other, and for 5.0 seconds, allows you to make 4 attacks against the target without range restrictions and with +100 attack speed.

Works like Force Staff when used on self or allies.

Allied Range: 800
Enemy Range: 400
Passive: Dragon's Reach
Increases attack range of ranged heroes by 140.

Hurricane Pike doesn't interrupt the target's actions.

Will not work on a unit inside Chronosphere, Duel, or Black Hole.

A legendary pike once held as royal sigil of the ancient wyvern riders.
