Item Details
Sange and Yasha
4,300
+ 16 Damage
+ 16 Strength
+ 16 Agility
+ 16 Attack Speed
+ 16% Movement Speed
Passive: Maim
Each attack has a 40% chance to reduce enemy hero movement speed by -26% and attack speed by -26 when used by a melee hero. Reduces enemy hero movement by -13% and attack speed by -13 when used by a ranged hero. Effect lasts 5.0 seconds.

Yasha-based movement speed bonuses from multiple items do not stack.

Dispellable.

Sange and Yasha, when attuned by the moonlight and used together, become a very powerful combination.
