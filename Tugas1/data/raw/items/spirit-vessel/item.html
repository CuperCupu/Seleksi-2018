Spirit Vessel
2,975
+ 250 Health
+ 30 Movement Speed
+ 1.0 Mana Regeneration
+ 2 All Attributes
+ 2.0 Armor
Active: Soul Release
When used against enemies, it reduces health by 4.5% of current health per second, and reduces HP regeneration and healing by 70%. Deals 20 damage per second.

When used on allies, it increases health by 1.0% of current health per second, and increases HP regeneration and healing by 20%. Provides 35 health regeneration per second.

Lasts 8.0 seconds.

Gains charges every time an enemy hero dies within 1400 units. Only the closest Spirit Vessel to the dying hero will gain a charge.
