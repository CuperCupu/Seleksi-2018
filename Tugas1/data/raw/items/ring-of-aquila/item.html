965
+ 10 Damage
+ 3 Strength
+ 9 Agility
+ 3 Intelligence
Passive: Aquila Aura
Grants 0.5 mana regeneration and 2 armor to nearby allies.

Radius: 900
Toggle: Aura
Deactivate to stop affecting non-hero units.

Does not stack with armor auras from Ring of Basilius or Ring of Aquila.

Multiple instances of Aquila Aura do not stack.

The ring of the fallen Warlord Aquila continues to support armies in battle.
