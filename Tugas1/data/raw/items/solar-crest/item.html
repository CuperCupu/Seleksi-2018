Solar Crest
2,625
+ 10 Armor
+ 1.0 Mana Regeneration
+ 20% Evasion
Active: Shine
When cast on an ally, grants them 10 armor and 20% evasion. When cast on an enemy, removes 10 of their armor and grants your allies 40% Accuracy on the target.

Removes the armor and evasion from the caster when used.

Cannot target magic immune enemies.

Range: 1000

Dispellable.
